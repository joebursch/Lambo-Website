Lambo's Story

I adopted Lambo in April 2013 from a Pet Supermarket in Wilmington, NC, during an adoption event for shelter dogs at risk of euthanasia. About 8 dogs were lined up in cages, all appearing sad and lethargic. Amidst them, there was Lambo, standing out with his wild, fun, and energetic demeanor. Despite feeling empathy for all the dogs, I knew that Lambo was the perfect match for me.

Lambo was quite a handful, with incidents of going to the bathroom on the walls, a lack of commands, and overall chaotic behavior. Nevertheless, I cherished him deeply. Beneath his initial exuberance, Lambo revealed his sensitive, intuitive nature filled with love. With consistent training and the establishment of a routine, he transformed into the ideal dog.

Lambo enjoyed constant attention in a family deeply fond of animals. Between my family and my wife's family, our households were bustling with various creatures. We had four additional dogs, four cats, a bunny, and a turtle that Lambo found particularly intriguing.

Lambo was our little goop ball, always seeking cuddles, playtime, and perhaps a bit too much food. He was our most cherished companion, always there to lift our spirits on a rough day. The time we shared with him holds a special place in our hearts. Lambo found peace in November 2023 after succumbing to hemangiosarcoma.
